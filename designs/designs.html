connect

Connect aims to make connecting and networking with students, faculty, and alumni easier and more fluid. Making connections and networking in college can help students understand their major better, produce more effective work, and secure jobs after college. This process should be an easy one, but there is not a one-stop-shop for all connecting needs. Some applications provide this service well, but they lack settings that can help students narrow their search in finding meaningful connections and networks. Connect gives the student those missing features. After planning, testing, wireframing, and prototyping the Connect application, we have seen that our users not only find this application easy to use, but they also find it invaluable in their connecting and networking endeavors.
